About the training course

The online Medical Affairs Training Course, delivered by Mantra Systems, will open up unique opportunities to work in the medical device industry without leaving your existing clinical role.

You will learn essential principles of Medical Device Regulation and applied literature reviewing, providing you with skills and knowledge highly relevant to a career in medical affairs.

After finishing the video training, you will receive a certification of completion and have the option to complete a post-course exercise.

Mantra Systems advertises for full-time Medical Writers throughout the year. Applicants who apply for Medical Writing positions along with their post-course exercise, are well positioned as stand-out candidates for roles with Mantra Systems.

The on-demand video training course includes:

  • Detailed coverage of the EU MDR and medical device regulatory requirements
  • Practical exercises and guidance to apply knowledge to key tasks
  • Gain core skills for non-clinical career progression as a medical writer or in support of regulatory activities
  • Certificate of Completion awarded with 3 hours of CPD points credit
  • Post-course exercise provides advantages for job applications to Mantra Systems
